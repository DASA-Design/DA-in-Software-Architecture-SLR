# SLR Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria (IC & EC)

| ID | TYPE | CRITERIA | DESCRIPTION |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---|
| **IC-01** | Inclusion | *Date Relevance* | Published 2010–2020 (or 2010–2024 for update). |
| **IC-02** | Inclusion | *Text Availability* | Text fully available in one of the academic sources. |
| **IC-03** | Inclusion | *Document Type* | Labelled as *article*, *conference paper*, *journal*, or *proceedings*. |
| **IC-04** | Inclusion | *Title Keywords* | Title includes `"Dimensional Analysis"`, `"Dimensionless Coefficients"`, `"Π-Theorem"`, `"Software"`, `"Design"`, `"Tools"`, `"Development"`, `"Architecture"`. |
| **IC-05** | Inclusion | *Abstract Keywords* | Summary includes relevant keywords as the title. |
| **EC-01** | Exclusion | *No Title* | Document does NOT have a title. |
| **EC-02** | Exclusion | *No Authors* | Authors NOT listed in the document. |
| **EC-03** | Exclusion | *No DOI* | No Digital Object Identifier (*DOI*). |
| **EC-04** | Exclusion | *No Abstract* | No summary or abstract. |
| **EC-05** | Exclusion | *Is Book* | Labelled as a book or book part (e.g., chapter or section). |
| **EC-06** | Exclusion | *Not Readable* | Written in a language other than English or Spanish. |
